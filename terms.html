1. What ListingLens does

ListingLens reads publicly visible Amazon listing and review content in your browser. Pro users can send selected content to OpenAI or Google Gemini using their own API key to generate copywriting suggestions.

2. Free and Pro plans

The Free plan allows checking up to 10 different products per day, with no AI analysis. The Pro plan ($39/month) removes the daily product limit and includes up to 150 AI analyses per month.

3. Billing

Pro subscriptions are billed monthly by Polar. We do not store payment card details. You can cancel through the customer portal; access continues until the end of the current billing period.

4. AI provider usage

In Bring Your Own API Key mode, any OpenAI or Google usage costs are your responsibility and are billed directly by that provider.

5. Acceptable use

Do not use ListingLens to violate Amazon's terms, bypass access restrictions, automate purchases, automate login, or perform actions in Seller Central.

6. No guarantee of outcomes

ListingLens provides analysis and suggestions based on available data. It does not guarantee increased sales, reduced returns, or any specific business outcome.
