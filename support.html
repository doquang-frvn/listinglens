Why can't I see more than a few reviews?

Amazon sometimes limits how many reviews are shown in a browser session. ListingLens can only analyze reviews that are visible on the page. You can paste additional review text manually in the Reviews tab as a workaround.

Why does Analyze ask for an API key?

ListingLens uses your own OpenAI or Google Gemini API key for AI analysis — this keeps your data going directly to the provider you choose, not through our servers. Gemini has a free tier that doesn't require a credit card; get a key at aistudio.google.com/apikey.

I hit the Free plan limit. What now?

Free includes 10 different products per day. Upgrade to Pro ($39/month) for unlimited products and 150 AI reports per month — see the homepage for details.

Is my Amazon account at risk?

ListingLens only reads publicly visible page content — it never logs into your Amazon account. We still recommend using a separate, buyer-only browser profile when researching listings, rather than one signed into Seller Central.

How do I delete my data?

Open the extension's Reviews tab and click "Clear Reviews," or simply remove the extension from Chrome — this deletes all locally stored data.
